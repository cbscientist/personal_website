Debriefing

I'm working with this book here:

I struggle with the adoption of the word "bitch" by feminists, so for years I had avoided picking this up, until I stumbled across it from a lending library. Plus knitting is one of those pasttimes that calls up connotations of grandmothers and ugly sweaters, whether actually created by said grandmothers or by some hopelessly misguided young woman whose skills don't reach far beyond the garter stitch and who has yet to figure out that cheap yarn is usually cheap for a reason. Polyester isn't much improved by application of knitting needles and an unskilled hand.

But there are people who knit beautiful things, out of amazing materials, and it's that level of skill that I aspire to. Hence the birth of a project herein known as k2p2. Our objective: to learn to knit, perl, and all that, and to be able to contribute to the creation of beautiful things. Ultimately, this is what any craftperson aspires to...the perfect union of form and function.
